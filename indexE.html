How to play Maze of Colours

Sequential movements:

Use the arrow keys on the keyboard to move the character through the maze obeying the following color sequence: Blue, Red and Yellow.

If you step on the blue in the wrong order, you go back to the beginning.

Red takes away your heart.

Yellow will not let you through if it is not in the right order.

Goals:

Get a Q to answer a Quiz question! If you make a mistake, you will lose your heart, but you will not be obliged to answer everyone, so use your logical reasoning to answer everything or leave some behind.

The keys open the way for you.

If you take the trophy, you win the game!
